Overview

Open left panel

The Alchemist (Portuguese: O Alquimista) is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho which was first published in 1988. Originally written in Portuguese, it became an international bestseller translated into some 70 languages as of 2016.An allegorical novel, The Alchemist follows a young Andalusian shepherd in his journey to Egypt, after having a recurring dream of finding treasure there. The story is a modern variant of the type The Man Who Became Rich Through a Dream (Aarne-Thompson-Uther type 1645). Over the years there have been film and theatrical adaptations of the work and musical interpretations of it.
